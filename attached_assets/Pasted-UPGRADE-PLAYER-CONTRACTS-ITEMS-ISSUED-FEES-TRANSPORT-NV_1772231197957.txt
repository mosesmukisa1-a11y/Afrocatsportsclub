REFERENCE (NVF)
- Contract release fee may not exceed N$3,000 for transfer to any other club/team. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
- Transfer requests can include a transfer fee determined by NVF for the relevant year (if any). :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

GOAL
Under each Player Contract, implement:
1) Items issued to player (item name, qty, value, date received)
2) Membership payment tracking
3) Development fee tracking
4) Transport benefits tracking (value included in totals)
5) A Transfer Fee Calculator:
   - NVF transfer fee (configurable yearly table, since NVF may set it annually)
   - Contract release fee (capped at N$3,000 by NVF rule)
   - Plus total value of all items + transport + any unpaid fees (membership/development)
   - Output a final “Transfer Amount Due” breakdown (transparent and auditable)

TRANSFER CALCULATION RULES

1) NVF fee:
- Fetch NVFTransferFeeSchedule where year = nvfYear and feeType=INTER_ASSOCIATION_TRANSFER_FEE
- If not found, default nvfFee=0 and show warning: “NVF fee not configured for year”

2) Release fee:
- If contract has a termination/release fee value, use it BUT CAP at N$3,000 (NVF rule) :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
- releaseFee = min(contractReleaseFee, 3000)

3) Items value:
- Sum ContractIssuedItem.totalValue

4) Transport value:
- Sum transport benefits using frequency:
  - ONE_TIME: amount
  - WEEKLY: amount * number_of_weeks_between(dateFrom, dateTo or today)
  - MONTHLY: amount * number_of_months_between(dateFrom, dateTo or today)
  - PER_TRIP: amount * tripCount (allow user to input trips or store trips separately)
Provide a simple method:
- If dateTo missing, calculate up to transferDate.

5) Outstanding fees:
- membershipOutstanding = max(0, membershipFeeRequired - membershipFeePaid)
- developmentOutstanding = max(0, developmentFeeRequired - developmentFeePaid)

6) Total Due:
totalDue = nvfFee + releaseFee + itemsValue + transportValue + membershipOutstanding + developmentOutstanding
